2. Encrypting and Decrypting a Message:

  • Click on the 'Encryption' tab on the tab menu to open the Encryption Panel
  • Enter the message in the 'Message' Field.
  • Enter the KEY if Required. Fields with a '*' mark are compulsory to be filled for encryption or decryption process.
  • Click on 'Encrypt' or 'Decrypt' to perform the desired operation.

3. Encrypting and Decrypting a File:
 

  • Click on 'File I/O' tab from the Tabs menu.
  • Click on the browse button to Open the File Chooser dialog box.
  • Select the file to Encrypt or Decrypt.
  • Enter the KEY if Required. Fields with a '*' mark are compulsory to be flled for encryption or decryption.
  • Click on 'Encrypt' or 'Decrypt' Buttons to perform the desired action.
  • Select the 'SAVE' button. When the File Chooser dialog opens, select the location to store the New File.
